I'm a research fellow (formally a Florence Nightingale Bicentenary Research Fellow) in the Department of Statistics at the University of Oxford. I previously completed a postdoc with Chris Holmes and Arnaud Doucet working on conformal inference and causal machine learning. Before that I completed my DPhil as part of the AIMS CDT under the supervision of Arnaud Doucet and George Deligiannidis. My thesis covered several topics in (primarily) Bayesian machine learning, including Monte Carlo methods and deep generative modelling. Before they left Oxford, I also worked with Frank Wood and Hongseok Yang on topics in probabilistic programming.
