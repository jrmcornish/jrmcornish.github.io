Open positions

I'm looking for talented and ambitious PhD students, postdocs, and research assistants to join my group at NTU!

NTU is a great place to do research! It has a thriving and diverse faculty, and is extremely well-resourced, including in terms of computing resources (essential for modern AI research!).

For all enquiries relating to this, please email me at the following link:
rob.cornish.ntu.recruiting@gmail.com

PhD scholarships

I have PhD scholarships available. These positions are fully funded for 4 years, meaning both tuition fees and a living stipend are covered.

To apply, you should have a strong background in mathematics, statistics, and/or computer science. It's OK if you don't have a fully clear research plan yet (although it's great if you do).

If you're interested, please email me:

  • A short (1-2 paragraph) introduction about yourself
  • Your CV
  • Your academic transcripts
  • Your favourite piece of technical writing that you produced as lead author (e.g. an undergraduate thesis, a paper, or a report)

Intakes occur in January and August each year. To ensure time for the application process, please get in touch as soon as possible before your desired start date!

For general information the PhD programme at CCDS @ NTU, see here.

Postdocs

These positions are for 2 years. You should have a strong background in machine learning, with publications at conferences like ICML, NeurIPS, etc. You should also be interested in working on topics relevant for my research group.

If you're interested, please email me:

  • A short (1-2 paragraph) introduction about yourself and your research interests
  • Your CV
  • Your favourite paper that you have worked on as lead author
I am flexible with regards to timing, and applications will be considered on a rolling basis. Even if you're not ready to start right away, please feel free to get in touch!
